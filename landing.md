## üé® DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Brand Voice
- **Professional** but not stuffy
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Technical** but accessible
- **Regulatory-aware** - use correct terminology (IND, NDA, ICH, GCP)

### Color Palette
- Primary: Deep blue (trust, professionalism)
- Accent: Green (success, go, medical)
- Background: Clean white/light gray
- Text: Dark gray/black

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold, modern sans-serif (Inter, SF Pro, or similar)
- Body: Clean, readable (16px minimum)
- Code/technical: Monospace for any technical elements

### Imagery
- **Do**: Product screenshots, clean illustrations, data visualizations
- **Don't**: Generic stock photos of people in lab coats, cheesy handshakes

### Icons
- Use consistent icon set (Lucide, Heroicons)
- Medical/clinical themed where appropriate
- Simple, not overly detailed
